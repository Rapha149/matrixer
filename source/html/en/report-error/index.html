Report an Error

You found an error? Something doesn't work? You have suggestions for improvements or additional features?
Just contact me! I'm thankful for every reported issue.

How to report an error:

Send an email to mail@davidaugustat.com. Please include following details to make it easier for me the analyze the problem:

  • A short description of the issue
  • A screenshot
  • The text you tried to enter (so that I don't have to type it off the screenshot)
  • Your browser (and version if you can find it)
  • Your operating system

Non-error suggestions are also welcome. Thank you for your help!
